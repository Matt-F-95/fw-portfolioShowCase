Dev Biography

Creating high-performing, user-friendly, accessible websites and apps is a passion of mine, they truly do sell themselves! I graduated from Seneca Colleges Webmaster program(honours), hackerYou's part-time JavaScript & jQuery program, and George Brown Colleges Social Media Marketing program.

I have working experience in Social Media Marketing by creating & utilizing persona profiles, brand voices, business objectives, and community purposes.

I can put these together to analyse and provide an accurate and detailed report with your most benificial target-audience, audience, and competitors. The work I provide is of the highest quality, fully responsive, optimized for Search Engine Optimization, and tested on all devices.

I take great care in using best practices and keeping my code clean and well documented to make maintaining and expanding your website as your business grows as easy as possible.

Words aren't everything though, are they? As Linus Torvalds says "Talk is cheap, show me the code!".
